The school welcomes parents and students who wish to visit the school. Parents can also meet the Director Academics and Principal and teachers on any working day. Admission to any class is granted on the basis of the following:

Admission to any class is granted on the basis of the following:

  • Jr. & Sr. Kg., students are required to have an informal interaction with the pre-primary educators.

  • Students seeking admission to classes I and above will be required to write an evaluation test and also undergo an interaction with the educators.

  • All formalities and admission procedures should be completed by the parents in person.

  • Admission will be granted only on the receipt of the first term fees.

  • Admission forms are available at the school office on all working days.
