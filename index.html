About Us

Let us tell you about Krishna School

Krishna School Sankul – managed by MR. ATUL SAVALIYA, has been providing outstanding education and support to students across the globe. The educational approach at Krishna School Sankul is unique.

The educational approach at Krishna School Sankul is unique. Rather than structuring the learning around a strict curriculum, our educators focus on trying to create learning experiences that prepare students to be happy, balanced, productive, and innovative citizens of tomorrow.
